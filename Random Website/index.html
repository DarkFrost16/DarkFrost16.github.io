All About Unicorns

Introduction

Unicorns are magical creatures. Although most of them are presitne and perfect, some of them have flaws, which will be explained later. Unicorns can mostly be found with a magical horn at the tip of their head. Some special unicorns, called alicorns, even have wings.

Types of Unicorns

Atlantic Unicorns

Unknown to many people, Atlantis is just behind the gateway in the Unicorn Lands. Because of this, unicorns can live in this otherwise uninhabitable land. Atlantic unicorns are special in a few ways:

  1. They are a deep blue color (like the ocean)
  2. They have fins and gills
  3. They can breathe underwater
  4. Their magic is limited to controlling water
These adaptions allow the unicorns to live in Earth's deep oceans. Normally they are found in the Atlantic Ocean, as they cannot survive in the human polluted oceans for too long.
